| 7:45am | Walk upstream to **Myojin Pond** (~5km, 1-1.5hrs). Flat trail through primeval forest |
| 9:15am | **Myojin Pond + Hotaka Shrine** — still pond reflecting Hotaka peaks. 300 yen entry. One of Japan's most famous mountain views |
| 10:00am | Walk back to Kappa Bridge |
| 11:30am | Lunch near Kappa Bridge: **Kamikochi Imperial Hotel** for a splurge (curry/sandwiches) or simpler options |
| 12:30pm | Walk downstream to **Taisho Pond** (~3.5km, 1hr). Dead trees standing in misty pond = eerie, beautiful |
| 2:00pm | Catch bus back from Taisho Pond stop |

### Evening
| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 4:00pm | Back in Matsumoto. Rest, shower |
| 6:00pm | Dinner: different restaurant tonight — **Marushuu** for soba, or izakaya for local craft beer + Shinshu salmon |
| 8:00pm | Final night walk around illuminated castle |

> **Bring rain jacket** (mountain weather changes fast at 1,500m). Wear proper shoes. Carry water + snacks.
